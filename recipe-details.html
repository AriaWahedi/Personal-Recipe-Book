Ingredients for Qabili Plow

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g lamb or beef
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 carrots, cut into thin strips
  • 1/2 cup raisins
  • 1/4 cup almonds or pistachios (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Vegetable oil

Instructions (Short Version):

  1. Cook meat with onions, salt, pepper, and cumin until tender.
  2. Boil rice until half cooked, then drain.
  3. Fry carrots and raisins in oil; set aside.
  4. Layer meat and rice in a pot, top with carrots and raisins.
  5. Steam on low heat for 20–30 minutes.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!

Ingredients for Bolani

For the Dough:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup warm water
  • 1 tablespoon oil (optional)

Prepare the Filling:

  • 2 cups chopped gandana (leek or chives)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon balack pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chilli flakes
  • 1 tablespoon oil (to soften the gandana)

For Cooking:

  • Oil for frying

Instruction:

Mix flour and water to make dough, prepare a vegetable filling, roll the dough, fill and fold it, then fry until golden and crispy.

Ingredients for Manto

Ingredients:

  • 500g ground beef or lamb
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 teaspoons cumin
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 teaspoon salt (for dough)

Instructions (Short Version):

  1. Prepare the dough by mixing flour, water, and salt; knead until smooth.
  2. Cook the ground meat with onions, cumin, pepper, and salt.
  3. Roll the dough and cut into squares, place filling inside, and fold into dumplings.
  4. Steam the dumplings for 30-40 minutes.
  5. Serve with yogurt or tomato sauce.

Ingredients for Chocolate Cake

Ingredients:

  • 1 and 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

Instructions (Short Version):

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C) and prepare cake pans.
  2. Mix dry ingredients in a bowl.
  3. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla; mix well.
  4. Add boiling water slowly and stir.
  5. Pour into pans and bake for 30–35 minutes.
  6. Cool, then frost and serve.
